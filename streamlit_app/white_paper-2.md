# Streamlining Hospital Finances: An Innovative AI Solution to Counter Denials, Underpayments, and Payment Delays

## Executive Summary
The healthcare industry is grappling with financial concerns such as denials, underpayments, and delayed payments, leading to noteworthy revenue leakage. Traditional Robotic Process Automation (RPA) solutions struggle to keep pace with the intricacies of evolving contracts, updating charge masters, and modifying billing and CPT codes, thus creating financial inefficiencies. This paper introduces an innovative AI solution, the AI Agent, that synergizes knowledge, skills, and large language models to effectively address these challenges and streamline hospital finances. This AI solution has the potential to save significant time and revenue for hospitals, offering an excellent Return on Investment (ROI).

## Introduction: The Crisis in Hospital Finances
Hospitals, today, are increasingly bearing the brunt of financial challenges due to denials, underpayments, and payment delays from insurance companies. A study by the Advisory Board reveals that hospitals lose approximately 3.3% of their net revenue due to denials. Furthermore, underpayment is another significant issue, with over 80% of hospitals reporting underpayments from Medicare and Medicaid.

The payment collection process in healthcare is quite lengthy, averaging over 50 days. This protracted duration places additional strain on hospital finances, creating an urgent need for a solution that can address these challenges effectively and in a timely manner.

## The Need for Automation: Overcoming Complexities
Healthcare billing is a complex process that demands specialized domain knowledge. Traditional RPA solutions often fail to cater to the intricate facets of changing contracts, updating charge masters, and revising billing and CPT codes. There is an emergent requirement for a solution that can scale and adapt to these complexities while ensuring accuracy and efficiency in billing processes.

Moreover, the resolution demands an automated system that can handle the magnitude of the task at hand, while also being adaptable to the dynamic nature of the healthcare industry. An AI-driven solution can potentially fulfill these requirements, offering a promising solution.

## Unveiling the Solution: The AI Agent
In response to these challenges, we propose an innovative AI solution - the AI Agent. This AI Agent is not just a single entity, but a synergy of multiple AI agents, each possessing specific skills, such as Electronic Data Interchange (EDI) skills, contract skills, and knowledge of billing codes. These agents individually tackle specific tasks, for example, one agent might specialize in understanding and interpreting complex contracts, another in updating charge masters, and yet another in interpreting and mapping between International Classification of Diseases (ICD) and Current Procedural Terminology (CPT) codes.

When these agents come together, they form a comprehensive solution able to handle the billing process, identify potential denials and underpayments, and take proactive corrective actions. By leveraging the power of AI, the solution can read and comprehend complex contracts, leading to significant time and revenue savings.
